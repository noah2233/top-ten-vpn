If you’re concerned about privacy and security, installing a top virtual private network (VPN) on your browser should be one of the first steps you take. It extends private networks across public networks, allowing you to encrypt and hide your IP address and data.

There is a wide selection of VPN suppliers on the market, but if you’re using Mozilla Firefox – one of the world’s top 3 web browsers – you're going to need to find a VPN that includes a Firefox plugin. Many of today’s top VPN providers include a Firefox plugin, and allow you to take advantage of their features while using the browser.
