Another benefit of using a VPN for Australia is that it can get you around geo-blocking restrictions. Is it Legal to Use a VPN in Australia?

It is 100% legal to use a VPN in Australia. It is also legal to use a VPN to stream content from overseas, as Prime Minister Turnbull confirmed in a recent statement on his website: “The Copyright Act does not make it illegal to use a VPN to access overseas content. While content providers often have in place international commercial arrangements to protect copyright in different countries or regions, which can result in ‘geo-blocking’, circumventing this is not illegal under the Copyright Act.”
